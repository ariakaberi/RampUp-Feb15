Dope Loafers, originally born Ariel Kohn Akaberi, was born and grew up in Long Island, NY. As a teenager he grew up listening to Eminem, Dead Prez, Tupac, Blue Scholars, The Roots, Immortal Technique and various other political and concious rappers. After graduating from SUNY Albany he moved to Queens and then eventually Brooklyn. There he found other local rappers and learned honed his craft and differentiate his style.
